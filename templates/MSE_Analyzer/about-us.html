About Us

This website was created for the purposes of the homework assignments at the course Design and Architecture of Software at FINKI (FCSE) 2024/2025.

The purpose of the app is to provide general analysis of the Macedonian Stock Exchange, showcasing general trends and patterns, allowing users to get a clean grasp over the history of the market situation.

This in turn helps users plan and strategize future investments with a clear goal and a transparent view of past mistakes and successes.

 

Made with the help of:

  • django
  • Flask
  • requests
  • beautifulSoup4
  • ta (Technical Analysis)
  • pandas
  • MongoDB Atlas
  • Bootstrap
